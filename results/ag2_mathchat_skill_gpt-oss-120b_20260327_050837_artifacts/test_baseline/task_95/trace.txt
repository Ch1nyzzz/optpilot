### 1.  A first translation property  

Put \(m=0\) in \(P\):

\[
f\bigl(f(0)+n\bigr)+f(0)=f(n)+f(0)+2014
\]

hence  

\[
\boxed{f(n+f(0))=f(n)+2014\qquad\forall n\in\mathbb Z}\tag{1}
\]

Denote  

\[
t:=f(0)\qquad(\;t\neq 0\;),
\]

so (1) says that a shift by \(t\) adds the constant \(2014\).

### 2.  Expressing \(f(f(m))\)

\[
f\bigl(f(m)\bigr)+f(m)=t+f(3m)+2014 .\tag{2}
\]

Write  

\[
f(x)=2x+h(x)\qquad (x\in\mathbb Z) .
\]

\[
\begin{aligned}
2(x+t)+h(x+t)&=2x+h(x)+2014\\

\end{aligned}
