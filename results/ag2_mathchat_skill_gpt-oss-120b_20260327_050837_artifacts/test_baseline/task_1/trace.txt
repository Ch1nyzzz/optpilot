We are looking for a multiset \(L\) of positive integers satisfying  

1. \(\displaystyle\sum_{x\in L} x = 30\);
2. The *unique* mode of \(L\) is \(9\) (so \(9\) occurs more often than any other value, and at least twice);
3. The median of \(L\) is a positive integer **not** appearing in \(L\).

The median being an integer that is not a member of the list forces the list length to be **even**.  
If the sorted list is  

\[
a_1\le a_2\le\cdots\le a_{n},
\qquad n\text{ even},
\]

the median is  

\[
M=\frac{a_{n/2}+a_{n/2+1}}{2},
\]

which must be an integer, and \(M\) must differ from every element of the list.

The problem asks for  

\[
\sum_{x\in L} x^{2}.
\]
