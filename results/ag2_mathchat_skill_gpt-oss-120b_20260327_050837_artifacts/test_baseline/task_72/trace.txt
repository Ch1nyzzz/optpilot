### 3.  A key elementary inequality

\[
\boxed{\;
\frac{k+1}{v}-\frac{k}{u}\le\frac{2}{v-u}\;}
\tag{2}

holds.

\[

\]

\[
k\delta^{2}-(2k-1)v\delta+(k+1)v^{2}\ge 0 .
\]

\[
\Delta = (2k-1)^{2}v^{2}-4k(k+1)v^{2}=v^{2}\bigl(1-8k\bigr)<0
\qquad(k\ge 1),
\]

hence the polynomial is always non‑negative, which proves (2).
