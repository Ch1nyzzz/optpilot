primes \(2\) and \(5\).  
Hence for a positive integer \(N\)

\[
\frac{N}{c\,m}\ \text{is short}\iff 
\frac{c\,m}{\gcd(N,c\,m)}\text{ has only the prime factors }2,5 .
\]

\[
N_t = 10^{t}-1 .
\]

denominator after reduction are those that already divide \(c\,m\) and are
different from \(2,5\).  
Let  

\[
Q(c,m)=\frac{c\,m}{2^{\alpha}5^{\beta}}\qquad(\alpha,\beta\ge 0)
\]

be the part of \(c\,m\) that is **coprime to \(10\)**.

\[
\frac{N_t}{c\,m}\text{ is short}\iff Q(c,m)\mid N_t .
\]

\(Q(c,m)\) does **not** divide any \(N_k\) with \(k<t\).
