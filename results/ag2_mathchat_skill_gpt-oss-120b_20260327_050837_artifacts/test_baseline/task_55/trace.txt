3. **Evaluate each option**

   - **Early adopters**: Possess credibility, social connectivity, and openness to change; they persuade the early majority and set the stage for broader diffusion.  
   - **Middle adapters** (often called “early majority”): They adopt after the early adopters have demonstrated success; they are important but usually follow the lead of early adopters rather than initiate it.  
   - **Late adapters** (late majority/laggards): Adopt only after the change is well‑established; they are not the drivers of effective change.

4. **Select the best answer**  
   The segment most responsible for initiating and sustaining effective community change is the **Early adopters**.
