Because the logarithms are base \(2\),

\[

\frac{y}{xz}=2^{1/3},\qquad

\tag{1}

### 1.  Solve for \(z\)

From the first two relations of (1),

\[
x=\sqrt2\,yz,\qquad 
y=2^{1/3}\,xz .

\[
y=2^{1/3}\bigl(\sqrt2\,yz\bigr)z

\[
