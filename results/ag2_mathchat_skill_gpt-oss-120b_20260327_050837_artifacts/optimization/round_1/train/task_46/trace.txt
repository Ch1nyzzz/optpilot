   \[
   (2w)^{2}+w^{2}=125.

   \[
   4w^{2}+w^{2}=125 \quad\Longrightarrow\quad 5w^{2}=125.
   \]

   \[
   w^{2}= \frac{125}{5}=25 \quad\Longrightarrow\quad w = \sqrt{25}=5.
   \]  
   (We take the positive root because a length cannot be negative.)

5. **Find the length**  
   \[
   L = 2w = 2 \times 5 = 10.
   \]

6. **Compute the area**  
   The area \(A\) of a rectangle is \(A = L \times w\):  
   \[
   A = 10 \times 5 = 50.
   \]

7. **Select the matching option**
