\[
a_{n}=n^{3}+bn^{2}+cn+d\equiv d \pmod n ,
\]

\[
a_{2015}\equiv d\pmod{2015},\qquad   
a_{2016}\equiv d\pmod{2016}.
\]

Since \(2015\) and \(2016\) are consecutive integers they are coprime:
\(\gcd(2015,2016)=1\).

\[
a_{2015}\,a_{2016}\equiv d^{2}\pmod{2015\cdot 2016}.
\tag{1}
\]

a_{n}=n^{3}+d .

\end{aligned}
