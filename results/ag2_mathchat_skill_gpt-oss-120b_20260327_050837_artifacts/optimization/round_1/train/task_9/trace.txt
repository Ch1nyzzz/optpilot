### 1.  Geometry facts we will use  

1. **Power of a point** – for any point \(X\) and any line through \(X\) intersecting the circle at two points \(U,V\),

   \[
   XU\cdot XV = \text{Pow}_{\omega}(X).
   \]

   In particular, because \(DB\) and \(DC\) are tangents,
   \[
   DB^{2}=DC^{2}= \text{Pow}_{\omega}(D).
   \]

2. **Harmonic division** – if the tangents at \(B\) and \(C\) meet at \(D\), then the four points
   \((B,C;A,P)\) form a harmonic range, i.e.

   \[
   \frac{AB}{AC}\;:\;\frac{PB}{PC}= -1 .
   \]

   This gives a simple relation between the segments on the chords through \(A\) and \(P\).

3. **Law of sines** – for a triangle with circumradius \(R\),

   \[
   \frac{a}{\sin A}=2R,\qquad\text{etc.}
   \]
