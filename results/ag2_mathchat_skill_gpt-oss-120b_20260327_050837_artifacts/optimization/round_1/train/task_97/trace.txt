d_i=S_i-S_{i-1}=b^{2}-a^{2}=(b-a)(b+a).

\[

\[
d_{1}=1,\;d_{2}=3,\;d_{3}=5,\dots ,d_{k}=2k-1 .
\]

\{1,3,5,\dots ,2k-1\}.
